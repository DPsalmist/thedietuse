Who We Are

The Diet Muse is an online restaurant that provide different tasty delicacies to our customers. We help you prepare and deliver food to your doorstep anywhere and wherever you are in Lagos and Akure.
Our culinary skills is one that would make you forget going the restaurant way. When you go through our variety of dishes in our menu and have found the delicacy that suit your taste and wet your appetite, reach out to us by submitting your order.

  • One time booking from your smart phone.
